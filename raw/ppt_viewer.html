Random Graphs

Random graphs are mathematical structures that are generated by a random process. They are fundamental to the study of graph theory and have numerous applications in:

  • Network Science
  • Social Network Analysis
  • Complex Systems
  • Computer Science Algorithms

Key concepts include:

  • Erdős-Rényi Model
  • Connectivity Properties
  • Degree Distribution
  • Phase Transitions
